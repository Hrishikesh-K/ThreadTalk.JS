Features

{{- partial "loading.html" . -}}

Themeable

Make your comment engine your own. ThreadTalk.JS can be perfectly themed to match your website's look and feel. After all, you build the layout.

{{- partial "loading.html" . -}}

Lightweight

A comment system shouldn't bloat your website down. With a bundle size as low as ~9 KiB (brotli), and on-demand loading of comments, reduce network traffic with ease.

{{- partial "loading.html" . -}}

Secure

By leaving the heavy work to serverless functions, ThreadTalk.JS makes sure that the data is secure while going to and coming back from your database.

{{- partial "loading.html" . -}}

Forever free

Yes, there's just one price. Free, at least for the most part. There shouldn't be a price on user engagement. Also, it's open-source! Check out the source code on GitHub .
